ZITA

Zealous Information Technology Association

“Make the record or break the record”

To color the above words, we, the ZITAians, both make a record and break the record by the hand landed by our association ZITA - Zealous Information Technology Association. ZITA always flutters its feathers with a pride of its unanimity. The harmony of ZITA can be witnessed through the various functions thatwe conduct. Few of the functions are:

DECADE'16

“The future is developed from the past”

To satisfy this saying we had our alumni from the year 2006 who were the founders of this great association ZITA. Decennium, after a long period of ten years they shared their knowledge and experience with us.

CONNECT’16

It is in the hands of the mother bird to take care of its off spring to fly over sky successfully. Likewise, our alumni nourishes our knowledge with the latest happenings of the IT world which will be helpful to overcome the obstacles. We ITians may leave the college as graduates oneday, but we are always ZITAians. In a nutshell, CONNECT is a ZITA family get together.

ORIGIN

ZITA is an ocean of novelty and we ZITAians are its shining pearls. Every year, we dive beneath our ocean to show case our newly formed pearls, who are our bubbling juniors. ORIGIN is a special celebration for welcoming our juniors into our family of ZITA.

UZHAVAR URCHAVAM

The hands of ZITAians may play with mouse and keyboard but we never give up tradition despite the upcoming technologies. UZHAVAR URCHAVAM is a ceremony to salute to the farmers and our tradition. So many events were conducted for all the other department students. All events are tamil oriented.

UTSAHA’16

It is rendezvous for budding engineers, who are architects of future Technology. Utsaha’16 provides a platform for scholars to become proficient in emerging technologies. It’s a National level Technical Symposium. Many events like best manager, treasure hunt, googling code debugging etc were conducted. Magazine and souvenir will be released on that day.

GREEN COMPASS

Green compass is an association which guides students about organic farming and sustainability in agriculture, its benefits and creates awareness about environment,recycling of e-waste products,blood donation.
Green compass is to educate and inspire people.It provides free sapling,sows the seeds of helping mind to people by collecting and donating clothes and necessary amenities to orphanage.
